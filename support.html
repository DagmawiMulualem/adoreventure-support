Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get personalized recommendations?

Simply open the app, select your preferred activity category, and our AI will analyze your preferences to suggest the perfect experiences for you.

Can I save my favorite activities?

Yes! Tap the bookmark icon on any activity to save it to your favorites. You can access all saved activities in the Bookmarks tab.

How accurate are the AI recommendations?

Our AI analyzes thousands of reviews and local insights to provide highly accurate recommendations. The more you use the app, the better it gets at understanding your preferences.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal information with third parties. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

What if I find an error in the app?

Please email us with details about the issue, including your device model and iOS version. We'll investigate and fix it as quickly as possible.
